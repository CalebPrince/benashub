Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. We use a small number of them to make the site work properly.

2. Cookies We Use

We currently use only essential, first-party cookies:

  • A session cookie that keeps you logged in to your customer account or the admin panel.

Your shopping cart is stored locally in your browser (via localStorage), not in a cookie, and stays on your device only.

When you check out, you're redirected to our payment provider, Paystack, which may set its own cookies on its own pages to process your payment securely. Those cookies are controlled by Paystack, not by us — see Paystack's own privacy policy for details.

We do not currently use third-party advertising or analytics cookies.

3. Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Since we only use essential session cookies, blocking them may prevent you from staying logged in or completing checkout.
