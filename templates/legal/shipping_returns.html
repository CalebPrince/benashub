Shipping & Returns Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

1. Shipping Within Ghana

All products on Benas Hub ship within Ghana. Domestic shipping cost is calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and order size.

2. International Shipping

Only products marked "Ships Internationally" on their product page can be shipped outside Ghana. If your cart contains an item that isn't eligible for international shipping, you'll be asked to remove it before we can ship to an international address. Available destinations and rates are shown at checkout once you select a delivery country.

3. Order Processing

Orders are processed once payment is confirmed. We aim to prepare orders for dispatch within 1–3 business days; delivery time on top of that depends on your location and, for international orders, customs processing in the destination country, which is outside our control.

4. Returns

Because many of our products are organic health, wellness, and personal care items, for hygiene and safety reasons we can only accept returns of items that are unopened, unused, and in their original packaging, within 7 days of delivery. Please contact us before sending anything back.

5. Damaged, Incorrect, or Missing Items

If your order arrives damaged, incorrect, or incomplete, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order reference and photos where possible, and we'll arrange a replacement or refund.

6. Refunds

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method via Paystack. Processing times depend on your bank or Mobile Money provider.
